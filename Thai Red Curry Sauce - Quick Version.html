Thai Red Curry Sauce - Quick Version


Original recipe used chicken hearts. Tasty quick curry. [4-24-14]

2 shallots, roughly chopped
3 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
1 inch piece of galangal (or ginger), peeled & chopped
4-5 Thai red chili peppers, finely chopped
1-2 Kaffir lime leaves
1 stalk lemongrass, tender part, chopped
juice of 1 lime
1 tablespoon fish sauce (nam pla, thai style)
1 cup coconut milk
Pinch palm sugar (to taste)
Salt and pepper, to taste
freshly chopped mint leaves (or cilantro)
Oil


Direction:

In a blender, puree the shallots with the garlic, Thai red chili peppers, lime juice, nam pla and palm sugar.

In a large wok on medium heat, add 2 tbsp of oil and stir fry the galangal, kaffir lime leaves and lemon grass. Add the pureed shallot mixture and let cook 2-3 minutes.
[(Add the hearts, salt and pepper. Let cook 1-2 minutes], mix evenly and add coconut milk, let it simmer for 4-5 minutes. Garnish with mint leaves and serve over Jasmine rice.
