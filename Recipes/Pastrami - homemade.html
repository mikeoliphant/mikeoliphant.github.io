Pastrami - homemade


Pastrami:

4.5 lb corned beef brisket point
Soaked 2x for 30 min each
Dried
Rubbed:
2 T coarsely crushed black pepper
2 T coarsely crushed coriander seed
2 t garlic powder
2 t paprika
Smoked: pecan & alder, 200 - 240 F, internal: 165


Corned Beef:

Brining our own...
2.5 lb brisket flat

1 liter water
200 g kosher salt (about 3/4 cup)
3 t pink curing salt (20g)
100 g brown sugar
3 T pickling spices
1 T whole coriander seeds
1 T whole yellow mustard seeds
5 cloves garlic, smashed

mix, then refridgerate 5-7 days (up to 2 weeks)
