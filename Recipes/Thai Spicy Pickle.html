Thai Spicy Pickle




1/2 cup white wine vinegar
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
3/4 t chile de arbol paste
3/4 teaspoon sugar
1 cup small cauliflower flowerettes
2/3 cup finely shredded cabbage
1 to 1 1/2 cup cool water


Heat the vinegars to a simmer. Add the cauliflower and cook until tender, but not mushy. Remove with a slotted spoon. Blanch the cabbage for 2-3 minutes, in the vinegar, then set aside to cool 15-20 minutes.
Stir in the chile, sugar, cauliflower and dilute with water to taste.
