Korean Spicy Pork Lettuce Wraps




Marinade:

2 tablespoons gochujang
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sweet rice wine
2 garlic cloves
1 teaspoon minced ginger
1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
1 teaspoon Korean chile flakes
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
Black pepper


1+ pound pork shoulder, partially frozen
Lettuce leaves, for serving
Thinly sliced garlic, for serving


Mix the marinade ingredients in a medium sized bowl. Thinly slice the pork into pieces about 3 x 3 inches and 1/8- 1/4 inch thick. Add to the marinade being sure to coat both sides of each piece. Refrigerate several hours.
Grill the pork pieces over a high flame until just cooked through and slightly charred. Use scissors to cut the pork into bite sized pieces (1x1 or 1x2 inch). Let guests make small parcels with a piece of meat and a slice of raw garlic wrapped in a lettuce leaf. Enjoy with steamed jasmine rice.
