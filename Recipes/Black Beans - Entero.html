Black Beans - Entero


I usually use bacon fat.

12 ounces dry black beans
7 1/2 cups water
1 medium onion, diced
1 tablespoon flavorful pork fat
1 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt


Rinse beans and remove bad ones. Place water and beans in a dutch oven and bring to a boil. Skim if needed. Add onion and pork fat. Partially cover and reduce heat to low (2.4). Simmer 1 hour, then check beans. Check every 15 minutes until beans are cooked through, but not mushy. Add salt and simmer 15 minutes longer. Turn off heat, cover with lid and let cool to room temperature before storing in the refrigerator.
