Pizza dough - homemade


7/21/10 - dough was a quick rise style. Good texture, but not much flavor.

2012 - the last of Mike's rustic bread dough. Very wet, spreadable dough; use plenty of olive oil. Gets nice and thin.

2017 - Frank Pinello on Munchies:
1.5 cup water
2 T olive oil
10g yeast
1.5 T k salt
3.5 cups AP flour
Mix, put in fridge overnight
cut into 3 or 4 pieces
oven 500 degrees
10 - 12 minutes
