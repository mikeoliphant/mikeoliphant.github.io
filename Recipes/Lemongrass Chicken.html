Lemongrass Chicken




8 ounces boneless skinless chicken breasts, bite-sized pieces
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 large shallots, 1/8-inch slices
1 green serrano chile, thinly sliced
1 medium carrot, peeled and thinly sliced
1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
1/2 zucchini, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons fish sauce
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 teaspoon brown sugar
lemon grass puree (see note)
3 green onions, 1/2-inch slices
1 handful cilantro leaves
1 handful basil leaves


Cook chicken until nearly opaque. Add shallot, garlic and green chile. Stir and cook 30 seconds. Add carrot, celery and zucchini, cook 1-2 minutes until begining to soften. Add lemongrass puree. Mix together the fish sauce, lime juice and brown sugar - add to pan. Simmer 1-2 minutes. Quickly stir through the green onions, cilantro and basil leaves. Serve immediately over steamed short-grain rice.
