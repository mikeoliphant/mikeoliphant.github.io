Chicken Tagine




1 (3 1/2 pound) chicken, cut into 8 pieces, skin removed (back pieces set aside for stock)
1 onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon turmeric
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
2 pinches ginger
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup minced parsley leaves
1/4 cup minced cilantro
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 small pinch saffron
1 cup chicken stock
1/2 preserved lemon, diced


Marinate the chicken pieces in the onion, 1 garlic, spices and a little oil for 2 or 3 hours at room temp. Shake off onion, brown the chicken pieces and set aside. Add the spiced onion mixture to the pan; cook until soft. Add 2 more minced garlic cloves, cook 1 minute more. Return the chicken to the pot, add the herbs, lemon juice, saffron and chicken stock; simmer covered for about an hour. Remove the chicken pieces (keep warm) and add the preserved lemon then boil the sauce until thickened slightly (optional). Serve the chicken and sauce over plain or savory basmati rice.
