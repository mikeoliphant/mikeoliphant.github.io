Au Jus




1 1/2 cups beef broth
1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons minced onions
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
Freshly ground black pepper
2 t butter or fatty beef trimmings


If using beef trimmings, heat on medium to render the fat. Briefly saute the onion and garlic in butter/beef trimmings. Add remaining ingredients and simmer about 20 minutes or until reduced to 1 cup. Remove beef trimmings if used.
