Thai Chicken Sausage - Homemade


Use chicken or turkey!

3 pounds turkey thigh meat
1 pound turkey skin
1 large bunch (3 ounces) cilantro
1 bunch mint (0.5 oz leaves)
1 1/2 cups basil (1.8 oz leaves)
1 ounce minced ginger
1 ounce minced garlic
4 teaspoons black pepper
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1/3 cup fish sauce
2 1/2 spicy, red jalapenos (1.8 oz minced)
3 yellow/orange thai chilies with seeds


Lay skin on silpat lined tray and freeze. Then slice into sticks while mostly frozen. Return to freezer a bit before grinding.
