Pork Chops with Apricot Pan Sauce


The pan sauce is a simple chicken broth simmered in the pan once the chops were removed. A teaspoon of apricot preserves adds a little sweetness and complexity. Add salt and pepper to taste and thicken slightly with a corn starch slury.

Pork chops were brined for 90 minutes in the refrigerator then rinsed and allowed to rest 30 minutes. (Brine: 1 tablespoon kosher salt per 1 cup cold water plus a teaspoon or so brown sugar.)
