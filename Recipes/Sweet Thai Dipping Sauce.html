Sweet Thai Dipping Sauce


This sauce was very tasty as a dipping sauce for homemade Thai Chicken Sausage.

1/2 cup water
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon minced ginger
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 t minced chile (optional)
1/2 teaspoon ketchup (for color)
1 teaspoon cornstarch


Boil water and vinegar. Add everything except cornstarch. Simmer about 5 minutes. Make a slurry with 2 t water and the cornstarch. Add gradually until desired thickness is reached. Simmer another 30 seconds, then remove from heat and let cool. Store refridgerated.
