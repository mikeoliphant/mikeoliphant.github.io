Crispy Fried Chicken (KFC by A&C)


Use skin-on bone in pieces.

Brine O/N:

1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 tablespoon kosher salt
1 tablespoon chicken bouillon powder (asian style)
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1/2 tablespoon ground chile (hot)
1 tablespoon vinegar
Black pepper
3 cups water


Dry Mix:

2 c cake flour
1 c potato starch
1/2 kosher salt


Batter:

1 c Dry Mix
1 cup water


Brine chicken pieces overnight in fridge
Coat chicken pieces in batter
Dredge each piece in Dry Mix
Bring oil to 340f; fry pieces 5 minutes, remove and drain on rack
Bring oil to 355f; re-fry pieces 4-5 minutes (golden brown); drain on rack
