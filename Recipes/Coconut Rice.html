Coconut Rice




1 1/2 cups jasmine rice (rice cooker cups)
3/4 cup light coconut milk
3/4 cup water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 green onion, sliced
2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
1 fresh, red Chili, seeded and sliced


Cook rice in coconut milk and water and salt. When done, fluff. Just before serving, gently stir in the green onion, cilantro and chili.
