Vietnamese Carrot Relish with Jasmine Rice


Great with grilled meats.

2 tablespoons lime juice
1 tablespoon fish sauce
1 tablespoon neutral oil
1/2 tablespoon honey or sugar
1 tablespoon chopped mint
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 fresh chile, minced
4-5 carrots, shredded
Cooked jasmine rice


Mix the lime juice, fish sauce, oil and sweetener until honey/sugar is dissolved. Drizzle over the carrots and mix in the mint, garlic and chile. Let sit 20-30 minutes before serving over Jasmine Rice.
