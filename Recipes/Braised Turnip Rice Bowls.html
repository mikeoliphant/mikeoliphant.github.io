Braised Turnip Rice Bowls




1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 small star anise
1 teaspoon sichuan peppercorns
2 teaspoons minced ginger
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 chopped scallions (white and green parts separated)
5 ounces ground pork
10-12 ounces daikon radishes (peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces)
4 fresh shiitake mushrooms, diced
1/12 teaspoon shaoxing wine
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon dark soy sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons oyster sauce
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 cup water
2 teaspoons cornstarch (mixed with 1 1/2 t water)
Hot, cooked jasmine rice (to serve)


Heat oil in wok or pan over low heat. Add star anise and peppercorns. Infuse gently for 10 minutes. Scoop out and discard spices. Add ginger, garlic, and white parts of scallions and cook at medium high heat for a minute or so. Add the ground pork and stir-fry until browned. Add turnip and mushrooms and mix well.

Add shaoxing wine, salt, dark soy sauce, reg soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar and water. Mix well, cover and simmer for 15 minutes over medium heat until turnips are tender and translucent. Turn heat up to high, add the cornstarch slurry and cook for about a minute to thicken the sauce. Stir in the green parts of scallions and then serve over hot rice. Eat with a spoon.
