Peameal Bacon


http://www.epicurious.com/recipes/food/views/peameal-bacon-368929

4.5 lb pork loin
3 quarts water
1 c maple syrup - 300g
2/3 c k salt - 150g
2 T pink salt
10 peppercorns
1 T mustard seed
1 bay leaf
Chill 4 days
Pat dry, roll in:
1.5 c dried yellow peas, crushed
Chill uncovered 1 day
