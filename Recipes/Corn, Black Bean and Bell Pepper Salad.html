Corn, Black Bean and Bell Pepper Salad




1 (14 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
2 ears fresh corn
1 red bell pepper
2 green bell peppers
2 yellow bell peppers
2 small spanish onions (red)
2 garlic cloves, minced
6 tablespoons chopped italian parsley
1/2-3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
Salt
Pepper


Blanch the corn in boiling salted water for 1 to 2 minutes. When cool, cut the kernals from the cob and place into a medium bowl. Cut the bell peppers and onions into 1/4-inch dice and add to the corn. Mix in the garlic, parsley, olive oil and vinegar. Add salt and pepper to taste, mixing well. Can be prepared a couple hours in advance.
