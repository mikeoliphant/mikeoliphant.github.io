Not Papaya Salad




8 oz cucumber batons
3 ounces cherry tomatoes, halved
1 garlic clove
1-2 chiles (thai or serrano)
1 ounce (2 tablespoons) palm sugar
several lime wedges
1 tablespoon dried shrimp
1 ounce green beans, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons fish sauce
2 tablespoons peanuts, chopped


Using a large mortar and pestle, pound the garlic and chiles together until chunky. Add the palm sugar and lime segments; pound lightly. Repeat with the dried shrimp and then the green beans. Mix in the lime juice and fish sauce. Remove most of the solids, then add the cucumber batons; pound lightly and mix with the liquids. Add the tomatoes and peanuts; pound slightly to release some juices. Mix everything together in a medium sized bowl. Discard the lime skins if desired. Serve with jasmine rice.
