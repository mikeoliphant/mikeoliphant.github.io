Pickled Gai Choi (Chinese Mustard Greens)




4 1/2 ounces (approx. 1 1/2 cups) gai choi
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon sugar
1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar


Rinse gai choi and chop into approximately 1/2 inch pieces. Place in a bowl and sprinkle on the salt, sugar and vinegar. Mix well. Let sit at room temperature 5-6 hours, then refrigerate. Taste before use, and if desired give it a quick rinse and drain. Keeps several weeks.
