Stuffed Zucchini Blossoms


In progress....
Any color cherry tomatoes -- we used yellow

Crumbled feta
Cherry tomato, chopped small
Fresh bread crumbs
basil strips, a fair amount
Salt
Pepper
Extra virgin olive oil
Zucchini blossom


Remove pistol/stamin from blossoms. Mix feta, tomato, bread crumb, basil. Add salt and pepper plus a drizzle of olive oil, mix. Stuff blossoms with 1-2 teaspoons cheese mixture. Do not over stuff. Pan fry in lightly oiled pan over medium high heat until lightly browned on both sides. Serve warm, 3 or 4 per person.
