Salmon Packets


Serves 2.

2 (4-6 ounce) salmon filets, skin removed
2-3 (approx. 3/4 cup) plum tomatoes, diced
2 (approx. 1/4 cup) small shallots, finely chopped
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for oiling the paper
½ teaspoon dried marjoram or 2 teaspoons fresh
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
juice of ½ lemon (see *note*)
2 tablespoons white wine (see *note*)
parchment paper
