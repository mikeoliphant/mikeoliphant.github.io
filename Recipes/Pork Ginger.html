Pork Ginger




8 oz thinly sliced pork cutlet
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon mirin
2 t vermouth
1 tablespoon grated ginger
Canola oil
Finely shredded cabbage


Five minutes before cooking, marinate the meat in the soy, mirin, vermouth and ginger. Brown quickly in a lightly oiled hot pan. Serve with a mound of finely shredded cabbage along side.
