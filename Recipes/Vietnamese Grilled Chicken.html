Vietnamese Grilled Chicken


I typically do 5 or 6 thighs at a time. Adjust marinade volume as needed for quantity of meat.

2 pounds chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice
1 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoons black pepper
1 1/2 tablespoons neutral oil


In a bowl large enough to hold the chicken, mix everything except the chicken and oil.
Remove the bones and excess fat from the thighs. Add thighs to the marinade and coat each piece well. Add the oil, and mix again.
Let sit at room temp up to 2 hours, or place in the fridge 3-8 hours in advance.
Grill thighs on a medium hot grill, starting skin side down. Turn as needed until skin is fairly crisp and chicken is done - about 13-15 minutes.
Let rest 10 minutes, then slice the thighs into strips about 1/2 inch wide.
