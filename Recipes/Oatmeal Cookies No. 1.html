Oatmeal Cookies No. 1


From Meta Given's Modern Encyclopedia of Cooking, 1952. "Unusual - whole flakes evident"
I don't usually use the nuts.

2 cups AP flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 egg
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup butter, melted
1/3 cup lard, melted
2 tablespoons molasses
2 tablespoons water
2 c rolled oats
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup pecans


Mix flour, salt, soda and spices in medium bowl. In a large bowl, beat egg, add sugar and mix well. Add melted fats, molasses and water - beat vigorously. Add flour mixture and mix well. Stir in the rolled oats, raisins and pecans.
Drop mixture from a tablespoon onto a lightly greased baking sheet, far enough apart to allow for spreading.
Bake at 350 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes. Remove to a rack to cool.
