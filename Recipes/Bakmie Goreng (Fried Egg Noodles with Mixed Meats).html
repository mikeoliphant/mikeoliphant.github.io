Bakmie Goreng (Fried Egg Noodles with Mixed Meats)


2 nests of dried egg noodles
85 grams (3 ounces) prawns
85 grams (3 ounces) lean pork
85 g / 3 oz skinless boneless chicken meat
2 garlic cloves
2 green onions
2-3 sprigs coriander
500 grams (1 pound) chinese cabbage
2 tablespoons oil
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup stock or water
4 tablespoons ketjap manis
115 grams beansprouts (optional)


For the dip:

5 chillies
3-4 tablespoons soy sauce
