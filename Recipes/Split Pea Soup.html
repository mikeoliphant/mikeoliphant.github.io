Split Pea Soup


This recipe is from Sherry's childhood. It was obtained from a 4-H event.

1 tablespoon shortening
Bacon or hambone
2 1/3 cups (1 pound) split peas
8 cups cold water
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup diced carrot
1 cup diced celery
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper


Melt shortening and fry bacon in a large kettle or dutch oven. Wash and drain the peas. Put everything into the kettle and bring to a boil.

Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours. Serve hot with crusty bread.
