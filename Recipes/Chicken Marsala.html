Chicken Marsala




Boneless skinless chicken breast
Flour seasoned with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 garlic clove, minced
5 tablespoons butter, divided
1/4 cup sliced shallots
2 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms (crimini or white)
Flour or cornstarch
1 cup marsala
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 teaspoon kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper


Thinly slice chicken, lightly coat in seasoned flour, fry until golden in 3 tablespoons butter with minced garlic. Remove and keep warm.

Cook mushrooms and shallots in 2 tablespoons butter until limp. Add the marsala and chicken broth, bring to a simmer. Thicken slightly with flour or cornstarch slury. Add salt (to taste).

Nestle the chicken in the sauce, serve with boiled thin skinned potatoes or mashed potatoes.
