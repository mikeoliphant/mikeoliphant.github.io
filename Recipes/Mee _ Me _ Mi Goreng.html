Mee / Me / Mi Goreng


Proteins: shrimp, fish cakes/balls (sliced), chicken or beef, etc
Vegetables: gailan, bok choy, long bean, carrot, onion, green onion

Sauce::

1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons golden mountain sauce
1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
2-3 tablespoons kecap manis
1 tablespoon oyster sauce or 1 teaspoon sugar
2-3 t chile sauce (sambal olek)
1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)


100 g thai rice noodles, soaked until limp
3-4 garlic cloves
1 shallot
175-200 g proteins
150 grams vegetables
tofu stick, soaked in hot water (optional)
thin egg omelette (sliced in ribbons) (optional)
Water, as needed


1- Proteins
2- Garlic, shallot
3- Hard veg to half-cooked
4- Noodles and 1/4 c water
5- Sauce, plus water as needed to cook noodles
6- Soft Veg
7- Check seasoning
8- Egg and green onion
