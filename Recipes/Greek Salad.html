Greek Salad


Option 1 - Romaine lettuce, chickpeas, torpedo onion (farmer's market), tomato slices, sheeps milk feta. Dressing - lemon juice and red wine vinegar, s & p, fresh oregano, sugar (1/2 t), ev olive oil.

Option 2 - Tomato, red onion, radish, cucumber and cooked golden beets. Feta and vinaigrette as above.
