Nuoc Cham (Basic Dipping Sauce)


Use more water for basic dipping sauce, medium water for salad dressing and the smallest amount for Banh Cuon dipping sauce. Makes about 2/3 cup - plenty for two people.

Stir together::

39 ml lime juice (1.33 oz or 2.75 tbls)
39 ml fish sauce (1.33 oz or 2.75 tbls)
39-78 ml water (78 = 2.67 oz or >5 tbls)
1 1/2 teaspoons rice wine vinegar
20 grams sugar (1.5 tbls)
1 minced garlic clove (optional)
1 minced thai chile (optional)
