Polish Kielbasa


Adapted the 10 lb recipe to ~6 lbs meat. Used a little too much salt. Next time, try 5 tablespoons instead of 5 3/4 T.

4 pounds, 3 ounces trimmed pork meat -- 3/8-inch grind
1 pound, 8 ounces fatty pork belly -- 3/16-inch grind
5 3/4 tablespoons kosher salt -- NEXT time use 5 tablespoons
2 teaspoons sugar
1 1/4 teaspoons Insta-Cure #1
2 teaspoons coarsely ground black pepper
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/2 teaspoon fresh marjoram
1 1/4 cups non-fat dry milk
1 1/4 cups ice water
(generous 1/2 teaspoon ceyanne added to 3 pounds seasoned sausage meat)


Yield: about 6 pounds, 3 ounces seasoned sausage meat.
Smoked 3 hours 150-190 degrees, internal to 144, Hickory wood.
