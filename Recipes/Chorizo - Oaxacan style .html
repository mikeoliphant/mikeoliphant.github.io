Chorizo - Oaxacan style




2 pounds coarsely ground pork
3/4 cup white vinegar
28 g guajillo chile, seeded
35 g CA chile, seeded
30 grams chile de arbol, seeded
1/2 t hot chile powder
2 tablespoons paprika
1 t ancho chile powder
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
1 teaspoon ground coriander
3 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1 tablepoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon thyme
2 cloves minced garlic
2 cloves garlic blended with chiles
4 teaspoons kosher salt


Soak chiles, then blend with 2 cloves garlic. Mix everything together. Cook a little to taste and adjust with salt and other spices as needed.
