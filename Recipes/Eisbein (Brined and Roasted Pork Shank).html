Eisbein (Brined and Roasted Pork Shank)


Brined to 3% salt, for 2 weeks (used Cooking By Hand's calculation guidance).

Roasted at 325 degrees on a bed of sliced onions for about 4 hours. Basted with Amber Ale every 30-45 minutes (used about 10 oz). Target internal temp = 160*. Moved shank to a small sheetpan; returned to oven at 400 degrees to crisp up 15-20 minutes. Meanwhile, made gravy of stock, pan drippings and onions.
