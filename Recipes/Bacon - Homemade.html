Bacon - Homemade




Basic Cure Mix:

450 grams kosher salt
225 grams sugar
50 grams pink salt (curing salt #1)


Bacon:

Approx. 3 pounds pork belly
50 g basic cure mix (1/4 cup)
1 tablespoon crushed black peppercorns
3 garlic cloves, smashed
3 bay leaves, crumbled
2 tablespoons brown sugar


Sprinkle cure and seasonings over all sides of the pork belly. Place in a non-reactive dish. Cover and refrigerate 5-7 days. Turn meat and redistribute cure every other day. Place cured belly on a rack and refrigerate uncovered 1-2 days.

Smoke gently with a full water pan and apple wood at 150 - 200 degrees for about 4 hours or until meat is about 150 degrees.

Pull off skin (if present) and then chill before slicing.
