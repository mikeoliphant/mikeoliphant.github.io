Sushi Rice


3 Rice-Cups short grained rice
After cooking, add seasoned vinegar, while fanning to cool.

(Note: rice in picture is recommended by Kanako, but we haven't tried it yet.)



Seasoned Vinegar:

1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt (try 1 1/4 t first)
