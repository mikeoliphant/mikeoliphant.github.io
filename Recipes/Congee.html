Congee


sushi rice = short grain rice = calrose rice

Porridge - 2 portions:

75 grams Sushi Rice
3 cups water
1 or 2 coins ginger
2 t chicken bouillon powder (asian style)


Toppings:

a half-cooked egg
a couple spoonfuls of cooked protein : soy seasoned minced pork or shrimp
fried shallots
chopped green onion
chopped cilantro
At the table: seasoned soy sauce
At the table: white pepper

Rinse rice several times then soak in warm water for 30 minutes. Drain well. Coat rice with 1 t chicken fat. Add rice and ginger to boiling water in clay pot. Return to a boil, crack lid, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes. Add chicken bouillon and stir well. Simmer another 5-10 minutes until desired consistency. Divide between two bowls discarding the ginger. Add topping as desired.
(If using the eggs, cook them during the final 10 minutes: add eggs to just boiled water (off the heat), cover and let sit 5-6 minutes. Remove and let cool on counter 2-3 minutes before cracking into congee.)
