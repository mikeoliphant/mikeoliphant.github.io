North African Rubbed Roast Turkey




1/4 cup butter, softened
1 t ras al hanout (from new book)
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 lemon, juiced


Mix the lemon juice and all spices into the soften butter. Rub under the skin on the breast and thighs. Rub a little over the outside skin.

(Stuffing)

Roast 20 minutes at 400' F, then add 2 cups water to pan, cover with foil and roast another hour and a half at 350' F convection roast. Baste with broth (canned) at each 45 minute mark. Remove foil, roast another 30 minutes to brown.
