Polenta




1 part polenta or coarse corn meal
1 part milk
1 part cream or milk
1 part chicken broth, warmed
Cheese (gruyere)
Butter
Salt
Extra water as needed


Heat milk, stir in polenta. Slowly add warm chicken broth, then water until desired softness and consistency. Add cheese and salt to taste. Add a little butter for texture and richness (~1T).
