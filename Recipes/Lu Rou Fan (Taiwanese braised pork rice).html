Lu Rou Fan (Taiwanese braised pork rice)




1 1/2 pounds pork belly, skin on
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup rice wine (homemade)
1 star anise
1 (approx. 2 inch) cinnamon stick
2 garlic cloves, sliced or smashed
1 (1 inch) piece ginger, peeled and sliced
Water


Cut pork belly into 4 pieces. Marinate in soy and rice wine about 18 hours in the fridge.
Place in small sauce pan with remaining ingredients. Add water to almost cover the pork (1/4 cup or so). Cover and braise gently 2 to 2 1/2 hours, turning occasionally. When pork is tender, but not falling apart, remove from heat and let cool.
Refrigerate, and then cut pork into thick batons. Serve warm over rice or in a bao (steamed bun).
