Tomatillo Green Chili Sauce


From Adam.

10 tomatillos, peeled, rinsed and finely chopped
2 onions, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons flour
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 (7 ounce) can roasted green chilies, diced
3 tbsp salsa jalapena


Saute tomatillos, garlic and onion in olive oil until onions are clear. Mix in flour and broth until smooth. Add chilies and simmer for 30 minutes. Mix in salsa and simmer another 10 minutes.

(Enchiladas: baked chicken breast seasoned with garlic and lime juice, jack cheese, flour tortillas -- in soft casserole format.)
