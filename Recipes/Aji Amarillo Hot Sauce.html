Aji Amarillo Hot Sauce


Notes: perhaps too much yellow bell pepper. Difficult to get the fresh aji flavor and heat.

1/2 (2 ounce) medium white onion
1 (5 1/2 ounce) large yellow bell pepper, roasted and peeled
1 garlic clove
3/8 teaspoon kosher salt
9 ounces white wine vinegar
2 1/2 ounces aji paste
1/2 teaspoon sugar


Puree the onion, roasted bell pepper, garlic and salt in a blender. Simmer in a small sauce pan for 5 minutes with the vinegar, aji paste and sugar. Hot pack in sterile bottles.
