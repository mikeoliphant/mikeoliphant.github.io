Utapam


80 g basmati, 80 g jasmine, 40 g urad dal, 1/2 t fenugreek seeds, 1/2 t sugar --> soak in 275 g water overnight. Add add'l ~115 g water and 1/2 t sugar. Split into two equal portions and blend each to smooth, thick liquid. Combine in a bowl, cover loosely and let sit at RT overnight. Whisk in 1 t kosher salt.

Heat a fry pan over medium high heat. Lightly oil with a paper towel, ladle about 1/4 cup batter into pan and spread into a 8-10 inch pancake. Immediately sprinkle with chopped tomato, chopped onion, finely chopped green chile, cilantro and two or three pinches of spices (mixed cumin, corriander, turmeric, ground chile). When edges have set, carefully flip the pancake and cook another minute or so. Transfer to a plate and top with a runny-yoked fried egg.
