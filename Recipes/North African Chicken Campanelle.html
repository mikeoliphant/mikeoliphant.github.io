North African Chicken Campanelle




Olive oil
Diced onion
Minced fresh ginger
Cumin
Tumeric
Pinch cinnamon
Salt
Chopped cilantro
mario sauce (basic tomato sauce)
Small cauliflower, florets
Roasted red bell pepper (optional)
Boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces
Campanelle pasta


Blanch the cauliflower in salted water until just tender. Remove and set aside.

Saute the onion and ginger in olive oil until lightly browned and softened. Stir in the cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, and salt, then add Mario Sauce. Simmer 5 minutes, then add chicken pieces and cauliflower. Simmer about 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. Taste for seasoning and adjust.

Stir in cooked campanelle pasta and serve, sprinkled with cilantro.
