Potato Salad


Serves 4.

1 pound russet potatoes (3 medium)
1/2 cup sliced celery (2 stalks)
1/3 cup diced onions
1/2 cup mayonaise (Veganaise works fine)
3/4 teaspoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
several generous grinds black pepper


Simmer whole potatoes in salted water until tender (about 20 minutes). Place in cold water until cool enough to handle. Peel and cut into medium chunks (3/4 inch squares). Add to a bowl along with the celery and onions. Place in the fridge to cool to room temperature.

Mix together the mayo, sugar, vinegar, dijon, salt and pepper. Pour over the cooled potato mixture. Gently toss to coat. Add the eggs and mix gently, but thoroughly. Keep cool until ready to serve.
