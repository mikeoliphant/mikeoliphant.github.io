Fish Taco Batter - So Cal style


Oil: 350
Makes about 2 cups batter
(Detailed below is about 1/4 of original recipe)

4 t yellow Mustard
1 cup water (plus a few t more if needed)
1 1/2 cups AP flour
3/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 t Ajinomoto MSG
1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoons baking powder
Pinch mexican oregano


Goal: gluey, thick batter

Coat fish and then slowly lower into the fryer, swaying back and forth for about 6 seconds.
