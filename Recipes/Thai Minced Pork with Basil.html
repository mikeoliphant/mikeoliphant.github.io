Thai Minced Pork with Basil


A longterm fridge magnet standby.

2 t duck fat (or other)
1/2 (2 tablespoon) large shallot, minced
1 pound ground pork
1 tablespoon palm sugar
2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 cup thai basil


Pound Together:

1 teaspoon ginger
6 thai chilies
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1 tablespoon galangal
2 stalks lemongrass
2 kafir lime leaves
1/2 (2 tablespoon) large shallot
5 (1 1/2 tablespoon) garlic cloves


Heat the duck fat in a wok and stirfry the pounded mixture until fragrant. Add the Pork and minced shallot; stirfry until pork is no longer pink. Add the palm sugar and fish sauce; simmer a few minutes. Taste and adjust with salt, fish sauce or sugar. Stir through the basil and serve with jasmine rice and a thai boiled egg (boil egg 6 minutes, then ice water to cool slightly, return egg to warm/hot water to keep warm).
