Corny Pancakes


These are thin, tasty pancakes.

Wet: Whisk Together::

3/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vinegar
1/4 cream cheese
1 egg
2 tablespoons melted butter


Corny: Stir Into Wet, and let sit 10 minutes::

1/4 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup masa harina


Stir Together, then add to Wet-Corny::

1/4 cup AP flour
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
