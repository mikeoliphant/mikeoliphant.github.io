Hue Soup 2024 (Bun Bo Hue variation)


We use a meaty bone from a pork butt (shoulder).
Picture to the right is unrelated.

2 1/2 lb pork and bone
1 1/2 t annato seeds
1 tablespoon oil
1 lg onion, chopped in 1-inch pieces
1 1/2 qt water
2 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
25-30 g rock sugar
most of 2 stalks lemongrass, smashed
1 teaspoon kosher salt (or more)
1 scant t fine shrimp paste/sauce
approx 3 tablespoons Lemongrass-Chilli Oil (recipe follows)


Cover 2 1/2 lb meat/bone with water and bring to a boil. Simmer 1-2 minutes, drain and rinse.
In a 3 1/2 qt dutch oven, lightly toast 1 1/2 t annato seeds in 1 T oil until red-ish. Transfers seeds to a tea bag and set aside.
Lightly saute 1 lg onion (1-inch pieces) in red-ish oil.
Briefly sear meat chunks in same pan.
Add 1 1/2 qt water, bring to a boil, reduce heat to a simmer and skim if needed.
Add 2 1/2 T fish sauce, 25-30 gm rock sugar, top parts of 2 stalks lemongrass, smashed (bottom 2 inches saved for LGCoil) and annato seed bag.
Simmer a couple of hours, or until meat is very tender.
Add 1 scant teaspoon fine shrimp paste/sauce near the end of cooking, plus 1/2 of the lemongrass-chilli oil (see below).

Lemongrass-Chilli Oil:

2 1/2 tablespoons oil
1 t annato seeds
2 tablespoons minced lemongrass (white parts)
1 tablespoon shallots
2 minced garlic cloves
1 teaspoon chile flakes
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon fish sauce


Heat annato seeds in hot oil 30-60 seconds. Remove seeds and discard.
Add lemongrass, shallot, garlic and chile to the annato oil. Simmer 4-5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add sugar and fish sauce. Stir to dissolve the sugar.
