Sandwich Bread (2025)


Makes 2 loaves. I use store brand bread flour.
Modified Sally's recipe to fit my smaller loaf pans. Slightly less butter as well.

380 grams warm water
100 g warm milk
10 g instant yeast
40 grams sugar
20 grams salt
60 g soft butter
760 g bread flour


Mix wet ingredients and a couple cups of flour at low spead. Add butter, yeast sugar and salt, mix to a batter. Add remaining flour and mix until combined, 1-2 minutes.
Increase speed to medium and mix about 5 minutes. Increase speed to medium-high and mix another 3 minutes. Add additional water by the teaspoon or tablespoon if needed. Dough should stick to the bottom of bowl somewhat. Stop mixer and check dough for stretchiness and hydration. Mix an additional 3-5 minutes on medium or medium-high speed if needed.
Place dough in oiled bowl, turning to coat. Let rest 1-2 hours or until doubled.
Shape 2 loaves and place in oiled loaf pans. Let rise until about an inch above the pan edge, about an hour.
Preheat oven to 350f at least 30 minutes before baking.
Bake 35-40 minutes to an internal temperature of 195-200f.
Let loaves cool slightly (5 minutes) and then turn out of pans and cool on a wire rack.
