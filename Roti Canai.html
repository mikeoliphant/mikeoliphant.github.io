Roti Canai


Wonderful with dahls.

1 c bread flour (550 g)
1 (56 gram) egg
40 grams butter
1 tablespoon cold milk
1 1/4 cups water


Stand mixer:
mix 10 minutes
rest 5 minutes
mix 10 minutes

Shape into 10 balls, coat with butter, cover and refrigerate overnight before rolling, spreading and pan-baking.
