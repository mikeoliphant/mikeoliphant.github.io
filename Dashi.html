Dashi




20 g (4x5 inches) kombu
25 g (3 cups) bonito flakes (katsuobushi)
1 liter water


Place the water in a medium sauce pan and add the kombu. Let soak at room temp for 30 minutes.

Bring to a boil very slowly. Just before the water fully boils, remove the kombu.

Turn off the heat and add the bonito flakes. Return to a boil, simmer for 30 seconds, and skim off any skum. Remove from the heat and let the flakes sink.

Filter through a paper towel, squeezing gently. Use as needed and/or freeze in half-cup portions.
