Orecchiette with Salsa Fresca




1 1/2 pounds fresh tomatoes, diced
1 garlic clove, minced
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup fresh basil, finely sliced
2 tablespoons minced, fresh italian parsley
1 red serrano chile, seeded and minced
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
6 ounces orecchiete pasta


For the salsa fresca, mix together everything except the pasta (tomatoes through black pepper). Let sit at room temperature 30 minutes.

Cook the orecchiette in boiling, salted water for about 11 minutes, or until al dente. Drain well and let cool 5-10 minutes.

Put the cooked pasta into a large bowl and add the salsa fresca. Mix gently, but throughly to evenly coat the orecchiete. Let sit at room temperature another 30 minutes, then serve.
