Fish with Corriander Lime Butter Sauce




2-3 tablespoons Coriander Lime Butter
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1/2 shallot, minced
1 1/2 tablespoons white wine
Lime juice, to taste
Salt


Gently saute the shallot in the olive oil to soften. Add wine, simmer on very low 5 minutes. Add butter and lime (about 1/4 lime) and salt.
