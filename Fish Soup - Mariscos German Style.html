Fish Soup - Mariscos German Style


Saute onion and celery in oil. Used chinese soup spoons to measure tomato sauce. Add a little water if needed -- taste first. Add fish at the last minute.

2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
2 canned tomatoes
5-6 tablespoons tomato sauce
1 1/2 cups shrimp/fish stock
1 1/2 cups chicken stock
2 - 3 small-medium potatoes, peeled, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
salt - 1 1/2 teaspoon?
Pepper
3 squirts taco shop hot sauce
3 pinches mexican oregano
White fish such as seabass and yellow tail
