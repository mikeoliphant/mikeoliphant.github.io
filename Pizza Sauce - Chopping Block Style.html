Pizza Sauce - Chopping Block Style


Makes just under 2.5 cups (two small pizzas).

4 ounces tomato sauce
7-8 ounces canned tomatoes, chopped finely
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon fennel
1/4 tsp chile powder
< 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1 tablespoon olive oil


Simmer ~5 minutes. Cool slightly before saucing pies.
