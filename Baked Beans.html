Baked Beans


Same recipe as Dutch Oven Pork and Beans -----

Bacon trimmings work well. If you don't have smoked duck stock, use chicken stock; add as needed for desired consistency. Works well in a camp cooker dutch oven.

Serves 5-6 as a side dish.

4-6 ounces smoked bacon, chopped into small cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
1/3 cup ketchup
2-3 tablespoons molassas
2 teaspoons dijon mustard
1/2-1 cup smoked duck stock
2 15-oz cans small white beans, drained and rinsed
1 15-oz can Pork 'n Beans
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt


Brown bacon, add onion and cook until softened. Add ketchup, molassas and mustard. Stir in the beans and 1/2-cup stock, simmer 10-15 minutes. Taste, adjust seasoning and add additional stock if needed (beans will thicken upon further cooking). Refrigerate now if making ahead. Place into a small dutch oven, cover and simmer (with hot coals or in a 350* oven) for about 1 hour.
