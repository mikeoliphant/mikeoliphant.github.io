Carnitas




2 1/2 pounds country style pork ribs
1 cup water
fresh orange juice from 1/2 large fruit
1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 1/2 teaspoons mexican oregano
2 teaspoons kosher salt
2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon garlic powder
4 smashed garlic cloves
3/4 onion cut into wedges
1 teaspoon black pepper


Trim most of the fat from the pork ribs. Put everything (including pork fat) into a dutch oven and mix gently. Simmer about 2.5 hours turning the ribs occasionally. Remove ribs from pot and place on a sheet pan. Defat the sauce (reserve the fat) and boil to reduce somewhat.
When ready to eat, anoint the ribs with some of the reserved fat and carefully broil to warm and brown the meat. Turn as needed.
Serve with all the sides: Guacamole, fresh pico de gallo salsa, refried beans, red rice, seasoned shredded cabbage (salt and lime), hot sauce and warm corn tortillas.
