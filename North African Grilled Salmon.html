North African Grilled Salmon


Serves 2.

1 garlic clove, minced
2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon coriander
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
1 pinch cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon olive oil

2 (4-5 ounce) salmon filets, skin on


In a shallow dish, just large enough to hold the salmon, mix together the first 9 ingredients. Place the salmon in the dish, turning to coat all sides. Marinate the fish, flesh side down in the refrigerator for 1 hour.

Heat the barbecue grill. When hot, grill the salmon skin side down for 6-8 minutes, then skin side up for about 4 minutes. Carefully remove the skin and serve at once.
