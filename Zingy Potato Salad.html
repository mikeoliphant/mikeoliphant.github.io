Zingy Potato Salad


This is apparently a German style potato salad and is best made in advance.

2 pounds yukon gold potatoes
2 cups thinly sliced sweet onions
1 bunch celery, sliced
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup chopped italian parsley
Salt
Pepper


Simmer potatoes until tender. Let cool, then cut into medium chunks. Place chunks in a medium bowl with the sliced celery and onion. Stir together the vinegar and oil, then drizzle over the potatoes. Toss gently, but thoroughly. Add the parsley and salt and pepper to taste. Let sit at least 1 hour, then serve cold or at room temperature.
