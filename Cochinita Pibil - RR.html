Cochinita Pibil - RR


From Robert Rodrigez on You-Tube.

Annatto seeds are very hard and cannot be ground in a mortor and pestle. A coffee grinder dedicated to grinding spices works well.

5 tablespoons annatto seeds
2 teaspoons cumin seeds
1 tablespoon black peppercorns
8 whole allspice berries
1/2 teaspoon whole cloves
2 seeded habanero peppers
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup vinegar
juice of 5 lemons
2 tablespoons salt
8 garlic cloves
Dash tequilla
5 pounds boneless pork butt, cut in 2-inch squares
Banana leaves


Grind the annatto seeds to a fine powder using a spice grinder. Repeat with the cumin seeds, peppercorns, allspice and cloves.

Place the liquids, salt, garlic, and powdered spices into a blender and blend well. Combine the marinade with pork chunks in a large ziplock bag. Refrigerate a couple hours or overnight.

Line a baking dish with banana leaves. Pour the pork and marinade into the dish and wrap with the leaves. Roast in a preheated, 325 degree oven for 4 hours. Remove from oven and let rest 20 minutes. Serve with plain rice and a simple green salad.
