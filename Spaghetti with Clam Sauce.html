Spaghetti with Clam Sauce


Bite-sized pieces of swiss chard can be added during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
Serves 3-4.

3 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for garnish
1/2 onion, sliced
Salt
2 garlic cloves, sliced
2 tablespoons finely julianed carrot or red bell pepper
3/4 cup canned plum tomatoes (squished) or mario sauce
3/4 cup dry white wine
1 can baby clams, drained with juice reserved
1/2 t chile flakes
2 tbsp chopped parsley


Put the oil and onion into a fry pan and bring to medium low heat. Add a sprinkle of salt and cook slowly until the onions are nearly carmelized (about 15 minutes). Add the garlic and carrot or bell pepper. Cook about 2 minutes to soften slightly. Add the tomato and wine, simmer for 20 minutes, then add the clam juice and chile flakes and simmer another 10 minutes. Stir in the baby clams and heat through, about 2 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Mix with cooked spaghetti and serve in large, warm bowls - garnish with chopped parsley and a drizzle of olive oil.
