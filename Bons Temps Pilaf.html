Bons Temps Pilaf




1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 stalk celery, chopped finely
1/4 lg onion, chopped finely
2 shallots, chopped finely
** Sweat 5 minutes
1 rice-cup basmati rice
1/4 rice-cup riso pasta
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
** Toast 10 minutes
1 1/4 cups chicken stock (low or no salt)
**
1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper
3 tablespoons lightly toasted sliced almonds
** Add green pepper and almonds 5-15 minutes before serving
