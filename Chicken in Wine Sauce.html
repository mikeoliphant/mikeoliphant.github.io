Chicken in Wine Sauce


The original recipe used chicken breasts on the bone, full-fat soup and sour cream, and sauterne wine. We added the extra mushrooms as well.
2009 - We've started making a cream of mushroom soup base from scratch.



6 skinless chicken thighs (boneless or bone-in)
1 teaspoon salt, divided
3 tablespoons flour
1 can reduced-fat cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup low-fat sour cream
4 ounces crimini mushrooms, quartered (optional)
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1 teaspoon paprika
Hot cooked rice, for serving


Place chicken thighs in 9x12 pan and sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Scatter the mushroom quarters around the chicken.

Combine the flour with 1/4 sour cream, stir until smooth. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt, remaining sour cream, soup and wine. Pour evenly over chicken.

Sprinkle almonds and paprika over the top. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees. Serve hot over cooked rice.
