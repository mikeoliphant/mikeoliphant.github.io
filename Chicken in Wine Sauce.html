Chicken in Wine Sauce


The original recipe used chicken breasts on the bone and sauterne wine. We added the extra mushrooms as well.
2009 - We've started making a cream of mushroom soup base from scratch.



6-7 skinless chicken thighs (boneless or bone-in)
1-2 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided
3 tablespoons flour
1 can cream of mushroom soup or our homemade recipe
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup sour cream
6 ounces mushrooms, in rough chunks
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1 teaspoon paprika
Hot cooked rice, for serving


Place chicken thighs in 9x12 pan and season with 1 teaspoon salt at least 8 hours ahead of time.

Combine the flour with 1/4 sour cream, stir until smooth. Add remaining sour cream, soup, wine and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Add an up to an extra 1 teaspoon salt if using homemade soup (sauce should taste well seasoned). Scatter the mushroom pieces around the chicken and pour sauce evenly over everything.

Sprinkle almonds and paprika over the top. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees (or 1 hour at 400). Serve hot over cooked rice.
