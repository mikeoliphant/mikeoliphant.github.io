Jasmine Rice with Vietnamese Carrot Relish




2/3 cup jasmine rice, rinsed
1 1/3 cups water
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon Asian fish sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons honey
1 tablespoon chopped mint
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 Thai chile, minced
4 medium carrots, peeled and coarsely shredded


Cook the jasmine rice.

In a bowl, combine the lime juice with the vegetable oil, fish sauce, honey, mint, garlic and chile. Add the carrots and toss to coat with the nuoc cham sauce.

Serve the carrot relish on the rice.
