Chicken in Wine Sauce - Homemade Sauce


2020-21 - This is a work in progress.

2 tablespoons butter
8 ounces mushrooms, small dice
2 shallots, minced or 1/2 cup onion and 1 garlic
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon dried sage
1/2 t ground corriander
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 tablespoons flour
6 ounces white wine, divided
6 ounces milk


Saute mushrooms and shallots/onion/garlic in butter until very soft, but not browned. Add herbs, salt and pepper. Mix in the flour, then deglaze with about 2 ounces wine. Add the milk and bring to a simmer to thicken. Cook about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in remaining 4 ounces wine. Let cool slightly before continuing with main recipe.
