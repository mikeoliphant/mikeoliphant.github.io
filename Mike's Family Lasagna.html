Mike's Family Lasagna




1 pound ground beef
2 (7 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
Freshly ground black pepper
8 ounces fine egg noodles
1 tablespoon sugar
3 ounces cream cheese
1 cup low-fat sour cream
6 green onions, chopped
1 cup shreded cheddar cheese


In a fry pan, brown the meat over medium-high heat. Mix in the garlic powder, salt, pepper, sugar and tomato sauce. Simmer for 20 minutes.

Cook the noodles in boiling salted water until just tender, about 7 minutes. Drain.

Blend together the cream cheese and sour cream until smooth. Stir in the green onions.

Lightly grease a 9 x 13 inch pan. Layer half the noodles evenly in the pan and top with a layer of half the cream cheese, followed by half the meat sauce. Repeat. Sprinkle the cheddar cheese over the top and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Lightly brown the cheese under the broiler, then let rest 5 minutes before serving.
