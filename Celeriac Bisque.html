Celeriac Bisque




For the soup:

Extra virgin olive oil
Shallot
3/4 large celery root
1 large yukon gold potato
1 c turkey stock
1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
1/4 c cream
lemon juice from 1/2 lemon
Kosher salt


For the garnish:

Pancetta, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
1 pinch red chili flakes
1 tender, inner celery stalk
1/4 cup celery leaves
Thinly sliced shallot
Extra virgin olive oil


Saute shallot in olive oil. Simmer celery root, potato and 1/2 teaspoon salt in poultry stock until tender. Add the sauteed shallot to the vegetables and puree with hand-blender. Add cream, lemon juice and salt to taste.

Simmer the celery stalk along with the celery root and potato until soft. Remove the stalk and dice it before pureeing the soup. Alternatively, simmer in salted water on its own, then drain and dice. Gently pan fry the pancetta with the chili flakes until the cubes are crisp.

To serve the soup, ladle a generous portion into warmed, shallow bowls. Top each with a few celery leaves, and sprinkles of the tender celery stalk, sliced shallots and pancetta cubes. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and serve with basic crostini along side.
