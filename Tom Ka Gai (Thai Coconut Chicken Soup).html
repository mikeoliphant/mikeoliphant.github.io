Tom Ka Gai (Thai Coconut Chicken Soup)


Serves 4. Fairly rich - perhaps reduce the quantity of coconut milk. Very quick to make. Do not eat the lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves or chiles. Remove chilies at anytime during cooking if the soup begins to become overly spicy.

2 cups homemade chicken stock, unsalted
1 1/2 cups coconut milk
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 pound chicken thighs, cut into bite sized pieces
1 stalk lemongrass
10-15 rounds galangal
4-5 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces
3-4 chilies, cut into large pieces
2 tablespoons fish sauce (divided)
1-2 teaspoons palm sugar
150 grams oyster mushroom
2-2 1/2 tablespoons lime juice


Bring stock to a boil, add chicken and salt. Simmer 10-15 minutes. Smash the lemongrass and cut into 2 inch pieces (discard top green part). Add coconut milk, lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, chilies, palm sugar and about 1 tablespoon fish sauce. Simmer 3-5 minutes. Add mushrooms and simmer another couple of minutes or until mushrooms are just cooked. Taste and add more fish sauce if needed. Add about 2 tablespoons lime juice and taste again. Offer fish sauce and lime at the table.
