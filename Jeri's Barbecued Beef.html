Jeri's Barbecued Beef




1 lb beef, cubed
1 pound pork, cubed
1 can tomato soup
1 onion, diced
2 tablespoons sugar
1/3 cup vinegar
1/3 c worchestershire sauce
1 cup water
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
Celery seed (optional)


Place meat into a casserole dish. Mix remaining ingredients together, then pour over the meat and stir to combine.
Cover with foil. Roast 2 1/2 to 3 hours at 350 degrees. Mash into shreds with a fork. Serve on toasted hamburger buns with potato chips and soda.
