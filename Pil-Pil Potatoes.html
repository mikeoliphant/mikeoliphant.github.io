Pil-Pil Potatoes


Use a 10 inch fry pan with lid.

1 tablespoon butter
1/2 large onion, sliced in rings
Fresh thyme
3 medium yukon gold potatoes, peeled and sliced
Olive oil
Kosher salt
Black pepper
2 tablespoons white wine


Melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and thyme; cook to soften and begin to color. Add the potatoes and toss with the onion. Drizzle with additional olive oil to moisten, sprinkle with salt and pepper. Add the wine and cover. Reduce the heat and simmer about 18 minutes or until the potatoes are tender, but not mushy.
