Grilled Pork Tenderloin a la Mexicana




For the rub:

1 teaspoon ground ancho chile
1/2 teaspoon ground chimayo chile
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon mexican oregano
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin


For the sauce:

6 plum tomatoes
1 poblano chile
1 jalape√±o chile
2 garlic cloves, unpeeled
1/2 onion
1 tablespoon olive oil
Kosher salt
3/4 cup chicken broth


Char the tomatoes and chiles, then peel. Roast the garlic until softened, then peel. In food processor, puree tomatoes, garlic and onion until nearly smooth. Add chiles and pulse until finely chopped, but not smooth. Heat oil in fry pan, add puree and cook about 10 minutes. Add chicken broth to loosen the sauce and salt to taste. Simmer gently 5 minutes.
