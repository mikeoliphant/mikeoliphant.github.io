Basic Roast Chicken & Potatoes


These potatoes are nicely gooey. Cook Red Potatoes in salted water for about 10 minutes, then cut into large chunks and add to the roasting pan (chicken has been roasting for 15-20 minutes. Toss to coat with chicken juices. Add onion quarters as well. Sprinkle with salt. Roast along side the bird for about 30 minutes.
