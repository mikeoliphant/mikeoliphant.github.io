Breakfast Pizza


We make a really tasty uncooked pizza sauce right on the cutting board and use it to top toasted english muffin 'crusts.'

Cutting Board Pizza Sauce:

Canned tomato, drained
Minced garlic
Ground fennel
Dried oregano
Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 pinch cayenne pepper
1 drizzle olive oil


Cheese
Ham
Mushroom
Onion
