Roast Pork




2 1/2 pounds pork loin roast
2 teaspoons kosher salt
Black pepper


Rub a generous amount of salt all over the pork roast. Let sit at room temp, about 1 hour.
Pre-heat oven to 400* F.
Pat roast dry with paper towel. Grind black pepper over roast. Rub on a little olive oil.

Roast meat for about 1 hour or until nicely colored and internal temp about 140-145* F. Rest 10 minutes before slicing. Use pan juices as a moistening/seasoning sauce at the table. (Juices may be salty - taste.)
