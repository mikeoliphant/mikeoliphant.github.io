Three Bean Salad




1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 cup fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch lengths
1 medium onion, diced
1/2 large green bell pepper, diced
2/3 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup sugar
Kosher salt (1/2 teaspoon?)
Freshly ground black pepper


Blanch the green beans in boiling salted water for 1 to 2 minutes or until just tender. Chill in ice water 5 minutes.

Dissolve the sugar in the vinegar. Mix all the ingredients together and let marinade one to two hours, stirring occasionally. Can be served chilled or at room temperature.
