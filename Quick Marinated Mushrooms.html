Quick Marinated Mushrooms


Note: the quantities are guesstimates. Had recorded 1 lemon, 4 white wine, 1/2 red wine vinegar.
Dec 2010 - quick cooked style with thyme, garlic, white wine vinegar (1/4 cup), white wine (1/4 cup), water (2 tablespoons), Extra virgin olive oil, salt and pepper, white button mushrooms plus a few crimini.

Small white mushroom
1 garlic clove, sliced
1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
1 tablespoon minced italian parsley
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (1 lemon)
1 cup white wine
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Extra virgin olive oil


Saute gently about 5 minutes, then let sit an hour to meld.
