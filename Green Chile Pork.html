Green Chile Pork


This dish was inspired by food eaten on a road trip through New Mexico and Arizona.

Country style pork ribs also work well.

3/4 pound pork shoulder, cut into 1-inch pieces
Mike and Sherry's Southwestern Spice Rub
Vegetable oil
1 onion, diced
2 (7 ounce) cans roasted green chilies, diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 can chicken broth
3 canned tomatoes, diced
Kosher salt


Sprinkle the meat with spice rub and brown in hot oil. Remove and set aside.

Saute the onion and garlic in oil. When softened, add the green chilies and cook another 5 minutes.

Return the browned meat to the pan and add chicken broth and tomatoes. Simmer until the pork is tender, about 1 hour. Season with salt to taste, serve with rice, black beans and salsa fresca.
