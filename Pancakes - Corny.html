Pancakes - Corny




Wet::

3/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vinegar
1/4 cup cream cheese
1 egg
2 tablespoons melted butter


Corny::

1/4 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup masa harina


Dry::

1/4 cup AP flour
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder


Whisk Wet ingredients together. Stir Corny elements into Wet. Let sit 10 minutes. MIx Dry ingredients together, then stir into Wet-Corny until just blended. Cook as normal with butter on medium high heat.
