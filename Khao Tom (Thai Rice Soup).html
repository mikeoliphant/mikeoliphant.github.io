Khao Tom (Thai Rice Soup)


Often served for breakfast. Shrimp can be substituted for the meatballs. I often dilute the chicken stock by about a third.
Serves 2.

2 cups chicken stock, unsalted
2 garlic cloves
1/4 teaspoon white peppercorns
4 cilantro stems
2 1/2 ounces ground pork
2 teaspoons fish sauce
1 teaspoon soy sauce
2 cups cooked jasmine rice
1 green onion, thinly sliced
1 teaspoon cilantro, chopped
Pickled mustard greens (optional)
2 eggs, cooked and singapore style (optional)
Freshly ground white pepper, for serving
